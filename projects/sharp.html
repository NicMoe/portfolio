My work.

Sharp Printer Comparison Wizard

See live project View code on Github

Problem

Sharp USA came to us with 40+ pdf documents that compares their Multi-Purpose Printers against their competitors. The comparison documents were very detailed provided a lot of value to IT professionals looking to upgrade their printers. Sharp wanted a way to present these documents to Spiceworks users.

Solution

I designed and helped develop a wizard to help users find the comparison document that is most relevant to their current printer use and needs.
