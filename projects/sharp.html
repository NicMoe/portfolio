Sharp Printer Comparison Wizard

See live project View code on Github

Problem

Sharp USA came to us with 40+ pdf documents that compares their Multi-Purpose Printers against their competitors. The comparison documents were very detailed provided a lot of value to IT professionals looking to upgrade their printers. Sharp wanted a way to present these documents to Spiceworks users.

Solution

We wanted to provide a way for users to sort through and find the comparison that was most relevant to their needs. We created a wizard experience that allows the user to narrow down the options based on what printer they currently have that they want to replace. This short 1 to 2 question survey would narrow down the comparisons list to ones that included their printer.
